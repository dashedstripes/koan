# Text

### Margin

```css

.m{t (top) | b (bottom) | l (left) | r (right) | x (horizonal) | y (vertical) | blank}-{025 | 05 | 075 | 1-8}

.m-3 === margin: 3rem
.ml-025 === margin-left: 0.25rem
.mx-1 === margin-left: 1rem; margin-right: 1rem;
.my-2 === margin-top: 1rem; margin-bottom: 1rem;

.my-sm-2 === margin-top: 1rem; margin-bottom: 1rem; /* (only on small screens) */

```

### Padding

```css

.p{t (top) | b (bottom) | l (left) | r (right) | x (horizonal) | y (vertical) | blank}-{025 | 05 | 075 | 1-8}

.p-3 === padding: 3rem
.pl-025 === padding-left: 0.25rem
.px-1 === padding-left: 1rem; padding-right: 1rem;
.py-2 === padding-top: 1rem; padding-bottom: 1rem;

.py-sm-2 === padding-top: 1rem; padding-bottom: 1rem; /* (only on small screens) */

```
